# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Project

## Overview

This project focuses on analysing coffee shop sales data to uncover customer purchasing patterns, sales performance trends, and operational insights. Using Excel for data cleaning, analysis, pivot tables, and dashboard creation, the project provides actionable recommendations to improve sales growth, customer engagement, and operational efficiency.

The analysis was designed to help identify high-performing products, peak sales periods, and customer behaviour trends that can support better business decision-making.

---

## Objectives

* Analyse overall sales performance and revenue trends.
* Identify top-performing coffee products.
* Understand customer purchasing behaviour and peak transaction periods.
* Explore sales trends across different times and categories.
* Build an interactive dashboard for business reporting and insights.

---

The dataset includes information such as:

* Product Type
* Product Detail

* Unit Price
* Total Sales
* Store Location
* Transaction Time

## Data Cleaning Process

The following steps were performed to prepare the data for analysis:

* Removed duplicate records
* Checked for missing values
* Standardised product names and categories
* Formatted date and time columns
* Created calculated fields for revenue analysis
* Organised data for dashboard reporting

---

## Key Insights

* Certain coffee products consistently generated the highest revenue.
* Morning hours experienced the highest customer traffic and sales activity.
* Product categories such as coffee and bakery items contributed significantly to total sales.
* Sales performance varied across different store locations.
* Customer purchasing behaviour showed clear peak ordering times.

## Recommendations

* Increase inventory during peak morning hours to meet customer demand.
* Promote top-performing products through targeted marketing campaigns.
* Introduce bundle offers for frequently purchased product combinations.
* Optimise staffing during high-traffic periods.
* Use sales insights to improve operational efficiency and customer experience.
